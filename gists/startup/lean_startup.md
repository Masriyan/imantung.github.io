# Lean Startup


Principles:
1. Entrepreneur are everywhere
2. Entrepreneurship is management
3. Validated Learning
4. Build-Measure-Learn Cycle
5. Innovation Accounting

Practice
- Cross-functional team
- Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
  - Concierge MVP
